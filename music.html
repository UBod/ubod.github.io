Ulrich Bodenhofer's Music

Education:

Basically none. I learned classical guitar when I was around 10, but I quit after only a few months. When I was 17, I started playing for myself using song books and chord charts. In 2000, I bought my first electric and started teaching myself more seriously using John Ganapes' great book Blues You Can Use. The last years, I have mainly been using PowerTabs from the Web to learn new songs.

Instruments:

  • Fender Stratocaster (blue standard model with maple neck and vintage-like hardware)
  • Fender Telecaster (all-black lite-ash model with maple neck and Seymour Duncan pickups; sometimes used with drop D tuning)
  • Squier Telecaster (Affinity Special Edition, butterscotch blonde with maple neck; tuned to open G)
  • Ibanez AS73 (brown sunburst)
  • Walden Natura D550CE (electro-acoustic with dreadnought body)
